About Global Cuisine Explorer

Our Mission

Global Cuisine Explorer is your gateway to discovering authentic recipes from around the world. We bring together the best of culinary traditions, making it easy for you to explore, learn, and cook dishes from different cultures.

Whether you're looking for quick weeknight dinners or elaborate weekend projects, our platform provides detailed recipes, cooking instructions, and nutritional information to help you create delicious meals at home.

APIs We Use

1 TheMealDB API

TheMealDB is a comprehensive database of recipes from around the world. It provides detailed information about meals including:

  • Recipe instructions and cooking steps
  • Ingredient lists with measurements
  • Recipe categories and cuisines
  • Video tutorials from YouTube
  • High-quality meal images

Website: www.themealdb.com

2 Open Food Facts API

Open Food Facts is a free, open, collaborative database of food products from around the world. This API provides:

  • Detailed nutritional information (calories, proteins, fats, etc.)
  • Ingredient lists and allergen information
  • Product images and barcodes
  • Nutrition grades (Nutri-Score)
  • Product categories and brands

Website: world.openfoodfacts.org
